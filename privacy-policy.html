JobTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
✅ We do not sell or share your data

What JobTrack Does

JobTrack is a Chrome extension that helps you log job applications with a keyboard shortcut. It saves application data to your personal Google Sheet and optionally creates events in your Google Calendar.

Data We Collect

JobTrack collects the following data when you log a job application:

  • Job title, company name, and job URL (scraped from the current page)
  • Application date and status
  • Notes you optionally add
  • AI-generated role brief, years of experience estimate, and top skills (derived from the job description text)

Where Your Data Goes

  • Your Google Sheet: All application data is written directly to a Google Spreadsheet that you own and specify. We never access any other spreadsheets.
  • Your Google Calendar: If you opt in, we create events in your primary Google Calendar for daily application counts, follow-up reminders, and interview slots.
  • Your device: Application data is cached locally in chrome.storage.local to power the popup UI. This data never leaves your device except to write to your Google Sheet/Calendar.
  • AI analysis proxy: The job description text is sent to our Cloudflare Worker proxy, which forwards it to Groq's API to generate a role brief, YOE estimate, and skills. No personally identifiable information is included. The job description text is not stored by our proxy.

Google OAuth Permissions

JobTrack requests the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • spreadsheets — to append rows to your Google Sheet
  • calendar.events — to create events in your Google Calendar

We only write data — we never read, modify, or delete existing data in your Sheet or Calendar beyond what JobTrack created.

Data Retention

Local storage can be cleared at any time by removing the extension. Data in your Google Sheet and Calendar is yours — delete it any time directly from Google.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sheets API & Calendar API — governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Groq API — job description text is processed to extract role insights. See Groq's Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Workers — our proxy layer. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

JobTrack is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Continued use of JobTrack constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
